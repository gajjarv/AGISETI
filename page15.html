Geostationary Satellites - Teaching Resources

This page provides the necessary teaching resources for the Geostationary Satellites lab, including the GOES-17 recorded data file and the GNU Radio Companion (GRC) flowgraph used for signal analysis. You can download these files to guide students through hands-on exploration of satellite communications, digital modulation, and signal processing. The provided GRC flowgraph enables students to visualize the power spectral density (PSD) of the transmission, measure bandwidth, and analyze modulation characteristics.
