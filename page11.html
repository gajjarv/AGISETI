Past Events

In-Person Workshop: Hat Creek Radio Observatory (May 2024)
In May 2024, we hosted 8 community college instructors from around the country for in-person workshop at Hat Creek Radio Observatory, home to the Allen Telescope Array (ATA). Participants had the unique opportunity to learn about the AGISETI curriculum, tour the observatory, and run live observations using the ATA. Instructors also gave talks on how they envision AGISETI fitting into their courses, what they'd like to see from the program, and how we can approach curriculum development to best meet their teaching needs. The hands-on experience provided valuable insights into how to incorporate real-world radio astronomy into community college courses.
